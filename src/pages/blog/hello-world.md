## Why I Built This

I've always wanted a personal website, but kept putting it off. Today I finally sat down and made it happen. I used:

- **Astro** — fast, modern, outputs static HTML
- **Tailwind CSS** — for styling without writing tons of custom CSS
- **GitHub Pages** — free hosting, easy deployment
- **Formspree** — for the contact form (no backend needed)
